The Definition of Paint


Since childhood he has been captivated by the act of creation. Plying materials to create new forms he has experimented with many mediums throughout the years. In present day it is the entrancing flexibility of paint or, more specifically, spray paint that has captured his imagination. Weilding cans and a surprising array of tools the Doc is discovering new ways to create expressive images.

His eclectic landscapes draw the eye and leave the viewer pondering. Just when you think you know what to expect he will surprise you with a new style all it's own. Keep you eyes peeled here for what's to come from Dr. Paint!
